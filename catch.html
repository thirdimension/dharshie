They'll Never Catch Us

Blurb review

September 26, 2023 Dharshwana

A new year rolls in.

The steckler sisters,

Are running.

To lower their times,

And from their problems.

Our oldest,

Stella is still recovering,

From lies and betrayal.

Hiding behind her skin,

She fights herself.

For survival.

To be the best.

Our youngest Ellie,

Lies under a blanket of insecurity,

Wishing upon a star,

To be better,

For it to finally be her year.

With both sisters on the course,

In purple gold Nikes,

Only time will tell,

Who’s the year it’s going to be?

Unless of course,

Competition walks in.

Mila Keene.

Jealousy encompasses,

The “star” Steckler sister.

She can’t be called a star anymore, can she?

While the other watches with admiration.

You learn that,

Jealously is something,

Rises from helplessness,

And roots from insecurities.

Yet, is often masked with,

Indifference and hate.

Then again.

What happens,

When the competition goes missing.

Potentially Murdered?

Fingers point to the “star” Steckler,

Who was purple with jealousy.

Who’s definitely had a mean streak?

Will the stecklers fight their battles,

Or will they be able to outrun their problems?
