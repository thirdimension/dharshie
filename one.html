One of Us is Lying

Blurb review

August 25, 2023 Dharshwana

Detention on the first day of school.

What a way to start the year,

For the five “delinquents” found,

With phones in their backpacks.

The Jock,

The Queen Bee,

The Nerd,

The Resident Bad-boy,

The tech genius,

With a deathly allergy

All five in one room.

Only four come out.

A bottle of peanut oil found.

Secrets become undone.

Relationships crack.

In the midst of doubt.

You learn that

words are stronger than sticks.

Because words spread.

Faster than a wildfire.

That perfection is simply a wall,

Which hides secrets.

The wall is fragile,

One jab and everything falls,

Like a lineup of dominos.

Even through it all,

You learn to keep your friends close.

But, your enemies closer.

So no blood coats your hand.

You learn to second-guess everything,

And everyone.

But, you learn to trust.

Those who are different.

To stitch the ripped seams,

Of the same cloth.

One of us is Lying,

Isn’t about finding the truth.

It’s about shattering the ideology of perfection.

And embracing the ones around you,

Through a poignant murder mystery.
