Ace of Spades

Blurb review

October 23, 2023 Dharshwana

Every year at Niveus Academy starts as a picture of uniformity.

Blue blazers. White shirts.

Red ties.

And Pristine white socks.

A sea of blondes and brunettes. With big egos.

But, not as big as their credit card limits.

Every high school has it’s queen-bee.

The one who’s a little better than most.

Who’s hair is straighter, and her makeup more immaculate.

he one who has more secrets, On her Prada bag carrying shoulders.

Yet, instead of our regular high school movie, She stars as our protagonist.

Chiamaka: The only black girl in the school.

So proud of her mountain of power that she, Fears falling off the edge e.v.e.r.y.d.a.y.

First day of school, She has an ace in her hand.

The position of power.

A prefect. Colleges will love that, She’s already the queen bee.

In a school where power gets you anywhere and everywhere.

Yet, you realize power is a dirty thing.

Born by broken promises, and destruction.

A force powered by secrets and blackmail.

All masked with cocky confidence.

It’s simply a setup.
